About me

Previously I worked at Software Engineering and Architecture Team (SEAT) research team at the University of Tulsa. We have built a statistical model that can predict software developers' personalities and emotion from their day to day conversation in their Agile Sprint. We used IBM Watson™ Personality Insights service and IBM Watson™ Tone Analyzer for our research.

I have obtained my MS in Computer Science degree from the University of Tulsa in 2019 December. I have received my MBA degree in Management Information Systems from University of Dhaka in 2016. I received my BS degree from American International University – Bangladesh in 2013.
